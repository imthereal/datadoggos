What is DataDoggos?

DataDoggos is a game centered around breedable, collectible, and oh-so-adorable creatures we call DataDoggos! (Except Steve. He's kind of ugly.)

Each dog is five-of-a-kind and 82.6% owned by you; it cannot be replicated, taken away, or destroyed, even if you hit your desktop with a sledgehammer.

What's the big deal?

DataDoggos is the world’s second-most popular game to be built on blockchain technology—the same breakthrough that makes things like Bitcoin and Ethereum possible. Bitcoin and ether are cryptocurrencies but Datadoggos are cryptocollectibles. If you're an a%$ and won't adopt a DataDoggo from a shelter, you can buy, sell, or trade your DataDoggo like it is a traditional collectible, secure in the knowledge that blockchain will track ownership securely.
